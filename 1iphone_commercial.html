MAIN FEATURES


RADIO

Best Clock Radio integrates the best podcast player and internet radio with access to over 35,000 music, sports, talk and community programmed stations from around the globe. The internet section has radio stations from around the world and now a UK section. The application can play music stored on your iPad, 1 of 13 soothing sounds to help you relax, tune into the TWIT Network or any mp3 stream. All can be saved to one of 5 preset buttons.

ANIMATED BACKGROUND

The background changes to reflect the time of day. At 12pm the sun is high and slowly sets to accurately reflect the sunset in your current location.
At night the moon is shown in the correct phase.

ALARM

Best Clock Radio features an innovative easy to set alarm with a copy-written interface. Simply touch the alarm face to set the time you want. Selecting the AM or PM finalizes and sets the alarm. Wake up to any Radio or music station programed to one of the radio buttons.
